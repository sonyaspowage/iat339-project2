About Us

Pop-Up [pop-uhp]: A temporary retail establishment where merchandise of any kind is sold. Ranging in length from a few hours to months.

Popup Vancouver is an awareness platform for popups to connect with people who are interested in purchasing from local retailers, participating in neighbourhood events and supporting local creatives.
