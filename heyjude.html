HEYJUDE SHOP

Curated Vintage Fashion

 

When hand-picking a collection we focus on quality, fabric, fit and how that item fits into the current season. With the rise in fast fashion and its wide spread availability, we felt inspired to offer unique pieces that were also eco-conscious and reduced our environmental footprint. There is something so personal about vintage and really lends itself to an individual creating their own unique style. We try to make our pieces as one-off as possible by reworking as much of our collection to keep it fresh and current.
