Chinatown Mall

Vancouver's Original Street Wear Popup

 

CHINATOWN MALL is not a craft sale or flea market, it's a concept pop up shop that aims to connect the best retail stores, small brands, designers, artists, artisans, curators and collectors with an engaged audience of internet kids, eager to find the rarest goods in an exciting, new environment.Each seasonal marketplace features a curated selection of the best local goods combined with collectors and curators selling off rare novelty goods and deadstock, vintage & almost new apparel, sneakers and accessories from the most coveted international brands and designers.
